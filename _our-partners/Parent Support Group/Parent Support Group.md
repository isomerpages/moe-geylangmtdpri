<h4>Chairman's Message</h4>
<p>The GMS(P) Parent Support Group (PSG) has come a long way since its humble
beginnings.&nbsp; We believe at the heart of every successful and fulfilling
parenting journey lies the support of a strong community, as well as communication
between all key stakeholders within the GMS(P) family, such as the School
Leadership Team &amp; Staff, PSG, Parent Volunteers (PVs) and Students.</p>
<p></p>
<p>In GMS(P), we believe every child’s journey through school is shaped not
only by the lessons they learn in the classroom, but also by the encouragement,
guidance, and values they receive at home. As parents, we are the first
teachers and lifelong role models for our children. Our involvement makes
a profound difference in building their confidence, resilience, and love
for learning.</p>
<p></p>
<p>One of the most meaningful ways for parents to foster bonding is through
active participation in school activities. Whether it is volunteering at
events, joining workshops, or collaborating on projects, these opportunities
allow us to build connections with one another, strengthen ties with teachers,
and show our children that learning is a shared adventure. When children
see their parents engaged in school life, they feel a deeper sense of belonging
and pride.</p>
<p></p>
<p>As partners in education, parents and the school work hand-in-hand to
create a nurturing environment where children can thrive academically,
socially, and emotionally. By collaborating closely with teachers and staff,
we help cultivate a learning environment that encourages curiosity, resilience,
and joy.</p>
<p></p>
<p>I warmly invite all parents to be part of this journey—not only for your
own child, but for the entire school community. Your presence, ideas, and
energy make a difference. Together, let us continue to build a supportive
network that uplifts families and empowers our children to reach their
fullest potential.</p>
<p></p>
<p>To find out more about PSG, do visit us at GMS(P) website or you can email
us at <a href="mailto:psggmsp@gmail.com" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">psggmsp@gmail.com</a>.
For enrolment, you can download the application form and submit it to our
PSG email. We look forward to your continuous support and participation.</p>
<p></p>
