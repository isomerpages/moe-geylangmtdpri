<p style="text-align: justify;">The objectives of the Form Teacher Guidance Period are to:  
  
</p><p style="text-align: justify;">a) provide quality interaction time between Form and Co-Form teachers with their&nbsp;students so as to strengthen Teacher- Student Relationship (TSR)&nbsp;  
  
</p><p style="text-align: justify;">b)&nbsp;equip students with social and emotional competencies to enable them to:<br>&nbsp;  
* better understand themselves (Self-Awareness) <br>
* learn ways to manage themselves (Self-Management) <br>
* better understand the people around them. <br>&nbsp;Example: family members and friends (Social Awareness) <br>
* learn ways to work with the people around them (Relationship Management) <br>
* make appropriate and responsible decisions (Responsible Decision Making)
