<h3>Background of the School Song</h3>
<p>The school song was written by Miss Mariam Jean Gruber, a renowned music
composer from the World Division of the Board of Missions of the Methodist
Church in 1975. Miss Susheela Daniel, daughter of GMGS's longest-serving
principal, Mrs S A Daniel, composed the music score for the school song.</p>
<p>The school song, which has been adapted over time, continues to inspire
all and reminds us to remain faithful to the Truths of God.</p>
