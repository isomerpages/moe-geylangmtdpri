<h4>CCA Overview</h4>
<p><strong>Our key motto</strong>
<br>1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Be Gracious
<br>2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Be Responsible
<br>3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Ready to serve
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Our mission</strong>
<br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><u>Grow</u> in science knowledge.
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Learn about how Science affects the Environment
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><u>Lead and inspire</u> others to care for the environment
<br>Be a environmental champions in school</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Serve</u> our school/community
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Share our knowledge on Environmental Science issues with our schoolmates
and community
<br>
</p>
<p></p>
<h4>Our CCA activities</h4>
<p>1) Work closely with Nparks to plan and conduct activities for schoolmates
during recess.
<br>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Be a Green Champion on World Water Day and Earth Day</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be Nparks Community in Nature (CIN) programme and design/lead a Biodiversity
trail in school garden.
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>2) Enrich in our Science learning experiences with investigative using
STEM activities.
<br>
</p>
<p>- Dunman High School interns who planned a Recycled car making with our
P5 and P6 in 2023
<br>
</p>
<p>- Conducting a mini Greenhouse making as part of NIE research for some
students
<br>
</p>
<p>- Making bridges, aluminum tray maze, during CCA
<br>
</p>
<p>- Partnering 1 team of Republic Polytechnic students in vermicomposting
investigation project
<br>
</p>
<p>- Workshops on Strawbees with quikbots
<br>
</p>
<p>3) Take care of our school garden
<br>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Water the plants
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Remove the weeds
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Plant new plants
<br>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pick up the litter at the garden
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
