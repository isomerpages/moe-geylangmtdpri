<h4><strong>ðŸŒ± 1. Green Champions &amp; Garden Projects</strong></h4>
<p>We work closely with <strong>NParks</strong> to care for and improve our
school garden. Our members:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Plan and conduct garden activities with NParks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Take part in <strong>World Water Day</strong> and <strong>Earth Day</strong> as
Green Champions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Join the <strong>Community in Nature (CIN)</strong> programme</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Design and lead a <strong>Biodiversity Trail</strong> in our school garden
to educate others about nature</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h4><strong>ðŸ”¬ 2. Hands-On Science with STEM &amp; Sustainability</strong></h4>
<p>We explore science through exciting, hands-on learning experiences such
as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Building projects like <strong>bridges</strong> and <strong>aluminum tray mazes</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participating in <strong>workshops</strong> on composting and programming
with <strong>Micro:bits</strong>

<p>Learning about <strong>sustainability</strong> through activities like using <strong>Design Thinking</strong> to
